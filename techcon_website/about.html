Our History

TechCon began over a decade ago as a small gathering of technology enthusiasts and has since evolved into one of the most respected global technology conferences. From humble beginnings in community halls, we now host thousands of attendees and feature some of the most innovative minds in tech.

Our Mission

The mission of TechCon is to inspire innovation, encourage collaboration, and foster the growth of technology leaders who will shape the future. We aim to provide a platform for sharing groundbreaking ideas and practical solutions to real-world challenges.

Notable Past Speakers

Over the years, TechCon has welcomed industry pioneers and thought leaders who have inspired thousands of attendees with their insights and expertise.

Ada Lovelace (Symbolic Honor)

Celebrated as one of the first computer programmers, Ada Lovelace symbolizes TechCon’s commitment to honoring the roots of technology innovation.

Sir Tim Berners-Lee

Inventor of the World Wide Web, Sir Tim shared his vision for a more open and accessible internet, inspiring generations of developers and innovators.

Grace Hopper (Symbolic Honor)

Known as the “mother of computing,” Grace Hopper’s pioneering work in programming languages continues to influence the tech world today.
